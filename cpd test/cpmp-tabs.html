OVERVIEW

The Construction Project Management Programme (CPMP) aims to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of construction project management and its application in the built environment. The programme covers essential areas of project management and introduces students to specific construction project management applications.

We offer a blended approach combining in-class and live synchronous sessions alongside interactive online materials. The involvement of both academic experts and experienced property professionals ensures a rich, practical learning experience tailored to Built Environment practitioners. The programme is presented at NQF Level 5 (first-year university level).
PROGRAMME OBJECTIVES
By the end of the programme, delegates will have a solid understanding of project management principles, including the Project Management Body of Knowledge (PMBOK) specific to construction, practical use of MS Projects, construction project cash flow, and risk factors in construction projects.
