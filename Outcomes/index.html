Programme Format
The programme is presented at NQF Level 5 (first-year university level). The duration is 3 days and features a blended format — a mix of in-class and live, synchronous sessions alongside interactive online materials and activities. This retains the personal connection of face-to-face programmes while offering the convenience of online learning.
